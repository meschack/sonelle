# 0002: Reader-First Desktop Layout

## Status

Accepted.

## Decision

The primary desktop layout is a reader-first workspace:

- left library/navigation rail
- center reader surface
- optional right inspector
- slim bottom audio rail

## Why

Sonelle is not a podcast app. The text is the primary artifact. Audio, learning tools, bookmarks, and export all orbit the act of reading.

This layout keeps the book visible and stable while still making playback and word lookup immediately available.

## Alternatives Considered

Podcast-style layout:

- Strong for browsing audio.
- Weak for long-form reading.
- Encourages oversized cover art and generic player UI.

Two-pane transcript/player layout:

- Clearer than podcast style.
- Still makes audio feel equal to or more important than the text.

Reader-first layout wins because the app's core promise is reading while listening, not listening with optional text.
